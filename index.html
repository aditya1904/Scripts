Product Description

Product Description

Are you in search of a stereo headphone that is lightweight but at the same time heavy in sound output? Your search ends over here as Sony has come up with a smartly designed stereo headphone for all you music lovers. Equipped with loads of features, the Sony MDR ZX110A Stereo Headphone is an amazing product to go for. You will simply love to have it as your companion. Sony, one of the renowned manufacturers of electronic products, have always delivered the best products. This time also the brand has manufactured a stereo headphone for the music lovers in an amazing way.

Features

Before purchasing the product, it would be better if you can get an idea about its features. Take a quick look:

  • The headphone comes with 30mm driver unit that offers crystal clear audio. Presence of this unit will give you the experience of live orchestra and will therefore be a great option for music lovers.
  • It also comes with a 1.2 metre cable that helps you in listening to music even from a distance without any difficulty.
  • It features a gold plated L shaped stereo mini plug
  • 24oHms impedance and 12-22000 Hz frequency response
  • 98 dB/mW sensitivity allows powerful playback from several devices.
  • The on ear headband offers an optimum fit. The pressure relieving pads of this headphone makes you feel comfortable while you listen to your favourite numbers.
  • The item weighs only 120gm and you can therefore carry the product wherever you go without any difficulty.
  • It is foldable. This makes it easier for users to carry it without any difficulty

Therefore, you can go for this product without any second thought. Shop this Sony headphone online and indulge yourself in the world of music.

From the Manufacturer

Neodymium dynamic drivers deliver precise sound

Lightweight 30 mm neodymium dynamic driver units enable the headphones to deliver a punchy, rhythmic response to even the most demanding track. Combined with a highly sensitive diaphragm, you'll be able to turn the headphones up louder—without the need for an amp—and still enjoy clear, precise audio across the spectrum.

Swivel folding design makes travel easy

Take these headphones anywhere you go. The headphones’ swivelling earcup design enables easy storage when you’re not using them, and enhances portability when you’re travelling. The earcups swivel flat, so you can pack them into a suitcase or a bag without worrying about them taking up too much valuable space.

Enfolding closed-back design seals in sound

The closed-back, enfolding design wraps around the ear, helping to seal in music and keep distractions out. Acoustics are reflected back towards your ears, so you hear even the subtlest of sounds. You'll also feel every beat, as the closed-back design helps to maximise the bass signatures in your music.

Wide frequency range for clear highs and lows

The wideband frequency range—spanning 12 Hz to 22 kHz—delivers deep bass, rich mid-levels and soaring highs. Hear the detail in every track and stay in-tune with all your music.

Cushioned earpads for total comfort

Listen in total comfort. These headphones have a self-adjusting headband and soft cushioned earcups that cover the whole ear. Enjoy the long-lasting comfort you need for listening to your favourite albums back-to-back.

3.0 out of 5 starsProduct not registering on Sony's website
By Raj Kumar on 14 July 2016
Colour Name: White Verified Purchase
The feel
The Headphone is really good to look at and can be folded to carried easily in bag and hence passes on the level of compactness. But, the headphone is too small to cover the ears completely and hence if you have used a good quality headphone before this then you wouldn't like its feel that much. It does start hurting the ears due to its hardness after some time.
The Sound
Sony is known for its sound quality so in this model too the sound quality is fine but still it is just fine without any possibility of adding any more adjectives. Nothing of the extraordinary types but looking at its reasonable price that is not something which can be said as a really negative attribute.
Product Registration
Now this is a sector in which the product is really dubious. I tried to register the product on Sony's website but it denied the registration saying that the serial number on the product was already registered and hence the product cannot be registered. The other thing which seemed dubious is that the model number mentioned on the box doesn't come in the supplied drop down list available on the website which seems to be fishy. I raised my concerns with Amazon care but they assured me that I'll get one year standard Sony warranty without registration too if I face any such issues and this is one claim which can only be tested when I face any such issue but this is something which is different as all such products purchased earlier from Amazon got registered easily.
So if you are thinking of buying this headphone beware that it looks much more appealing in pictures than it in reality however the product is not bad but still its not something I'd but again and product registration failure raises many doubts in the mind.

4.0 out of 5 starsThis product is worth every penny being spent!! Amazing product. True Value for money.
By Ajinkya Bhede on 28 July 2016
Colour Name: White Verified Purchase
This product is worth every penny you spend on it. At this price range, the sound quality it offers is exceptional. The trebles and the bass are well balanced. You can hear the rhythm of every vocals and instruments. I read some reviews that bass is not good and sound creaks sometimes. Well guys, What more you want from a ₹600 headphone? For those who want more bass they can use a good equalizer from playstore and enjoy bass boosted sound.And regarding the creaking sound there may be several reasons three of them are,
1) The product you received my be a damaged one. (I think you'd know what to do next! Simply replace it!)
2) The audio jack of the device you're using may be damaged. (Change device or use different one!)
3) The sound quality of song is terrible. (Now, you can't listen a 90kbps song and complain it's creaking! I recommend to use 190/250 or 360kbps at best!)

And many people also complain that the sound coming from headphones is not loud. Regarding this I would say that the loudness of the sound depends on the driver of device you're using. Simply put PCs have powerful sound card and Mobiles ain't much powerful. So that's obvious that on pc it would sound louder. (In mobiles too loudness of sound varies from phone to phone)

Now comes the build quality. The build quality of these headphones is also good, but it would have been better. Even tough with this build it should last for at least 1 to 1.5 years. And its obvious that it won't even last a week if you're swinging them around whenever you're carrying them!

So my final verdict on Sony MDR ZX110A headphones is that, if you are on tight budget and want to enjoy quality sound and build at a low price then this device is surely for you. Don't over think guys just go for e'm!Read more ›
